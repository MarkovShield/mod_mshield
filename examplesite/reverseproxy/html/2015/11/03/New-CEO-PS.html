Philipp Sieber replaces former CEO Ivan Buetler

November 3, 2015

Philipp Sieber (ETH MSc) is announced as the new CEO of Compass Security Cyber Defense AG and as such responsible for Hacking-Lab and FileBox, a secure file transfer solution. He is replacing co-founder and former CEO Ivan Buetler who is now stepping back and preparing for his hip replacement surgery.

In his previous job, Sieber was a senior software developer at Crealogix E-Banking solutions. This is a man who spent years in his former job devoting it to producing secure software. Sieber will now push Cyber Defense products to a new level.

"Luck we have with Sieber, he is a big fan of the Hacking-Lab platform and therefore the best match we could think of", says Ivan.
